Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions about our community

Joining a club is easy! Simply browse our club categories, find one that interests you, and contact us through this form or visit our office. We'll connect you with club leaders and help you get started. Most clubs welcome new members throughout the year.
Basic membership in most clubs is free for ARSC students. Some specialized activities may have small fees for materials or equipment, but we work to keep costs minimal and offer assistance when needed. We believe financial barriers shouldn't prevent anyone from participating.
Absolutely! We encourage students to explore different interests. Many of our members participate in multiple clubs. We'll help you manage your time and commitments to ensure you can fully enjoy all your chosen activities without overwhelming your academic schedule.
Our community is especially welcoming to new students! We have buddy systems, orientation programs, and smaller group activities to help you feel comfortable. Many of our current members started out feeling nervous, and our leaders are trained to help everyone feel included from day one.
Time commitments vary by club and your level of involvement. Most clubs meet once a week for 1-2 hours, with optional additional activities. We respect that academics come first and work with your schedule. You can participate as much or as little as works for your lifestyle.
While our primary focus is on ARSC students, we do welcome community members and students from other institutions for certain events and activities. Please contact us to discuss specific opportunities and how you might get involved with our community programs.
